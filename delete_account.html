Delete Your Account

Festival Toilet App

Follow the steps below to permanently delete your Festival Toilet App account and all data associated with it.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Festival Toilet App
    Launch the Festival Toilet App on your Android device and make sure you are signed in to the account you wish to delete.
  2. Go to Festival Resource
    From the main screen, open the side menu (drawer) and tap on "Festival Resource" to open the home page.
  3. Tap the three-dot menu
    On the top-right corner of the Festival Resource home page, tap the three-dot menu icon (⋮).
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
    From the menu that appears, tap "Delete Account" (shown in red).
  5. Confirm Deletion
    A confirmation dialog will appear listing all the data that will be removed. Tap "Delete Account" to confirm. This action cannot be undone.

Before You Delete

  • Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once deleted, your account cannot be recovered.
  • You will be signed out immediately and will no longer be able to log in with the same credentials.
  • Any content you have posted (reviews, posts, comments, chat messages) may be removed or anonymised.

What Data Gets Deleted

  • Your profile information (name, email, phone number)
  • Your toilet reviews, ratings, and uploaded photos
  • Your social posts, comments, and likes
  • Your chat messages and conversations
  • Your user-submitted toilet locations
  • Your push notification token and preferences
  • Your Firebase authentication credentials
  • Your locally stored session data and cached content

Need Help?

If you are unable to access your account or need assistance with account deletion, you can request deletion by contacting us directly:

Email: support@crapadvisor.com

Please include the email address or phone number associated with your account so we can locate and process your request. We will respond within 7 business days.
